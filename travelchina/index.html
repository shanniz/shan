Traveling China advices by Shan

  • Step1: Land China (Kinda neccessary for the all the followings hahahaa) :)
  • Install following Apps
    • Wechat - Must have messaging App in China
    • Pleco - English-Chinese Dictionary
    • Microsoft Translator - Translates English sentences to Chinese and vice-versa. (Must download offline database for Simplified Chinese language)
    • Baidu Maps - Help alot finding locations and buses/subways to get there.
  • Buy a sim card at the airport (should cost around 150 RMB with 1GB Internet)
  • Get a transport card from a subway station. Charge it with 100-200 RMB or anything according to your use
  • ... Voila... Mission accomplished :)
