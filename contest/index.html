About Contest

Universal Programming Contest - For the universe.

The BIT-UPC Programming Contest 2017 is the 1st instance of the annual programming contest series sponsored by BIT and Software Companies in Beijing. This year, the contest starts on March 25 2017 at 12:00 UTC. This is an open contest for students. Participaant exclude the contest organisers and members of the same group as the the contest chairs. Advance registration with entry fee is required. Contestants can register themselves as teams of atmost 2 persons. Any programming language(s) on any platform(s) can be used to solve the problems. There will be cash prizes and certificates for the first(winner) and second(runner-up) place teams. the first place team is “the discriminating hackers”, the team winning the judges’ prize is “a cool bunch of hackers”.
